What is a Stake Pool?

A stake pool is used to delegate your voting powers to a third party (the pool). The pool cannot steal your funds. Participating in a stake pool is perfect for people who wish to take part in Decred's proof of stake voting but can't due to:
Not wanting to keep a constantly unlocked wallet
Lack of a reliable/low latency Internet connection

The redudancy and geographic distribution of our servers reduce the chance of missed votes and increases your profits. Our fees are of {{ .PoolFees}}% of the reward. Staking in the pool is less expensive than a cheap VPS or at home, with much less vote latency and much less missed tickets.
